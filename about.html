About Us

Who We Are

The Public Trust was officially launched in 2025. It is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving Indian society in various fields such as social welfare, education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability. Our mission is to serve communities with integrity, compassion, and transparency.

Our Vision

We aim to create a just, inclusive, and sustainable society where everyone has access to essential resources and opportunities for growth.

Our Mission

  • Providing quality education to underprivileged children.
  • Ensuring healthcare accessibility for marginalized communities.
  • Promoting environmental sustainability through green initiatives.
  • Encouraging social welfare programs for those in need.
